About AniTA

AniTA is an intelligent teaching assistant platform designed to streamline the educational experience for both professors and students. Leveraging advanced AI technology, AniTA automates assignment grading while providing comprehensive course management features. Our platform uses AI to analyze student submissions against professor-provided rubrics, delivering consistent and detailed feedback in minutes. This allows educators to focus on what matters most: teaching and mentoring students.

How It Works

1

Course Setup

Professors create courses and invite students to join.

2

Assignment Creation

Educators create assignments with detailed instructions and grading rubrics.

3

Student Submission

Students submit their work through the platform before the deadline.

4

AI Grading

AniTA analyzes submissions and provides detailed feedback based on the rubric.

5

Review & Finalize

Professors and students can review AI feedback and grades.

Benefits

Save Time

Immediate Results

Organized Workflow

Keep all courses, assignments, and grades in one centralized location.
